Daily Read
Healing Happens in Service 5 min

The final stage of every recovery program: help others. Not because you're a saint — because service rewires your brain from "survive" to "contribute."

It shifts your identity from broken person healing to healed person serving.

Your pain has purpose. Your story will save someone else.

"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others." — Gandhi

Daily Action
Serve One Person varies

Your Assignment

Help ONE person today. Not for credit. Not for social media. Genuinely serve. Buy someone coffee. Listen to someone who's struggling. Share your story with someone who needs it.

  • Choose one person to serve today
  • Do it without posting about it
  • Notice how it changes your energy
  • Journal about how service felt
Today's Session
Gateway Wave 6 Track 2 — Expansion 30 min

Headphones required. Find a quiet place. Lie down or sit comfortably. Follow the guided audio.

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. Who did I serve today?
  2. How did it make me feel?
  3. What if this became my daily practice?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
