Morning Protocol
Wim Hof Breathwork + Cold Shower 10 min

Before you check your phone. Before coffee. Before anything.

Breathwork (5 min): Sit up straight. Take 30 deep breaths — inhale fully through the nose, exhale through the mouth. On the last exhale, hold your breath as long as you can. Inhale deeply, hold 15 seconds. That's one round. Do 3 rounds.

Cold Shower (30 seconds): At the end of your shower, turn the water to cold. Stay in for 30 seconds. Breathe through it. This activates your vagus nerve and resets your nervous system.

Daily Read
The Gut-Brain Axis 5 min

95% of your serotonin is made in your gut. Not your brain. Your gut.

Processed food, sugar, alcohol, and antibiotics destroy gut bacteria. Destroyed gut means anxiety, depression, brain fog, poor sleep.

You can't meditate your way out of a diet that's poisoning your nervous system.

Healing starts in the kitchen.

"All disease begins in the gut." — Hippocrates

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min
