Daily Read
What Big Food Doesn't Want You to Know 5 min

Seed oils (canola, soybean, sunflower) cause inflammation at the cellular level. They're in EVERYTHING — restaurants, packaged food, "health" food.

High fructose corn syrup rewires your hunger signals. Artificial sweeteners destroy gut bacteria. MSG is an excitotoxin that overstimulates brain cells.

Read labels like your life depends on it — because it does.

Eat real food: meat, fish, eggs, vegetables, fruit, nuts, seeds. If it has a commercial, question it.

"Let food be thy medicine and medicine be thy food." — Hippocrates

Daily Action
Clean Meal Prep 45 min

Your Assignment

Meal prep ONE day of clean food. No sugar, no seed oils, no processed ingredients. Document what you eat with photos.

  • Plan one full day of clean meals
  • Prep the food (no sugar, no seed oils, no processed)
  • Take photos of what you eat
  • Read labels on everything in your kitchen

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. What did I eat today? Was it real food?
  2. How did clean eating affect my energy and mood?
  3. What's the hardest food/drink to give up? (That's the one that owns you.)

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
