Daily Read
Your Shadow Runs the Show 5 min

Carl Jung said: "Until you make the unconscious conscious, it will direct your life and you will call it fate."

Your shadow is the parts of you that you buried to survive. The anger you swallowed. The sadness you numbed. The needs you pretended didn't exist.

Your pattern IS your shadow's operating system.

Daily Action
Shadow Journal 20 min

Your Assignment

Write: "The thing I don't want anyone to know about me is..." — fill 1 full page. Burn it or keep it. Your choice.

  • Write the shadow prompt — 1 full page minimum
  • Sit with the discomfort for 5 minutes after writing
  • Choose: burn it, tear it up, or keep it
  • Write 1 sentence about what you noticed
Today's Session
Yoga Couple Day 3 — Shadow Awareness 30 min

Going deeper into shadow work. The parts of you that run the show from behind the curtain.

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. What did writing that feel like?
  2. What shadow behavior showed up today?
  3. Did I judge myself? Can I choose compassion instead?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
