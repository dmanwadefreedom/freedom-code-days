Daily Action
3 Circles Exercise 20 min

Your Assignment

Draw 3 circles on paper. Inner circle: people who fuel you. Middle circle: people who are neutral. Outer circle: people who drain you. Set ONE boundary with someone in the outer circle today.

  • Draw the 3 circles — inner, middle, outer
  • Place every important person in your life
  • Set one boundary with someone in the outer circle
  • Write about how setting that boundary felt
