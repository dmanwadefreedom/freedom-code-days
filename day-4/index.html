Daily Read
The Inner Child Never Left 5 min

Between ages 0-7, your brain was in theta state — pure programming mode. Every fight your parents had, every time you were ignored, praised, shamed — it wrote code on your hard drive.

That code is still running.

Your inner child isn't a metaphor. It's a neurological reality. The patterns you run today? They're a 5-year-old's survival strategy in an adult body.

  • Read it out loud (yes, out loud)
  • Write what emotion came up during reading

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. What did your inner child need that they never got?
  2. How does that unmet need show up in your relationships today?
  3. What's one thing you can give yourself right now?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
