Daily Read
Sleep: The #1 Performance Drug 5 min

7-9 hours. Non-negotiable.

During deep sleep your brain clears toxic waste via the glymphatic system. Without it: memory loss, weight gain, weakened immunity, emotional instability, impaired decision-making.

Blue light from screens suppresses melatonin by 50%. No screens 1 hour before bed. Sleep in total darkness. Cool room (65-68 degrees).

This alone will change your life.

"Sleep is the best meditation." — Dalai Lama
