Daily Read
What Your Body Is Telling You 5 min

Chronic stress keeps cortisol elevated. Cortisol means belly fat, poor sleep, brain fog, low libido, weakened immune system.

Your body keeps the score. Trauma lives in the tissues. Tight hips equal stored fear. Tight jaw equals suppressed anger. Tight chest equals grief.

Movement isn't optional — it's medicine.

Daily Action
Walk and Write Your Values 30 min

Your Assignment

20-min walk outside. No phone. No music. Just walk and breathe. Then write your top 5 non-negotiable values (what you will NOT compromise on in life).

  • 20-minute walk — no phone, no music
  • Write your top 5 non-negotiable values
  • Identify which values you're currently violating
  • Choose one value to honor fully starting tomorrow
Today's Session
Yoga Couple Day 6 — Core Needs, Values, Non-Negotiables 30 min

Exploring your core needs, values, and the non-negotiables that define who you are.

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. What values am I currently violating in my life?
  2. What would change if I honored them?
  3. Where in my body am I holding tension right now?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
