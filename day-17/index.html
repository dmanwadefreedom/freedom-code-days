Morning Protocol
Full Morning Stack + 20-Minute Workout 50 min

Full morning stack first. Then 20 minutes of bodyweight work: push-ups, squats, lunges, plank, burpees.

No gym needed. Your body is the gym. Just move.

Daily Read
Exercise: The Real Antidepressant 5 min

30 minutes of exercise is as effective as SSRIs for mild-moderate depression (Blumenthal et al., 2007).

Exercise increases BDNF (brain fertilizer), boosts serotonin, reduces cortisol, improves sleep, and builds confidence.

Your body was designed to MOVE. Sitting 8+ hours a day is literally killing you.

Movement is not optional in this program.

"Movement is a medicine for creating change in a person's physical, emotional, and mental states." — Carol Welch
