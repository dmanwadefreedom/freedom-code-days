Defense Mechanisms

Stop the bleeding. Build the morning anchor. Get honest.

Morning Protocol
Gateway Wave 1 Track 3 — Advanced Focus 10 10 min

Before you check your phone. Before coffee. Before anything. Headphones required. Sit or lie down comfortably. Follow the guided audio.

Daily Read
Your Armor Is Killing You 5 min

Defense mechanisms: denial, projection, intellectualization, humor as deflection, people-pleasing, stonewalling, rage, withdrawal.

These kept you alive as a kid. But in adulthood? They destroy intimacy, block growth, and keep you stuck in the same loops.

The pattern you run IS your primary defense mechanism.

Daily Action
Identify Your Armor 20 min

Your Assignment

List your top 3 defense mechanisms. For each one, write: "I use this when I feel ___." Share with your partner or accountability person.

  • List your top 3 defense mechanisms
  • Write what triggers each one
  • Identify the feeling underneath the armor
  • Share with someone you trust
Today's Session
Yoga Couple Day 5 — Defense Mechanisms and Coping 30 min

Understanding the defense mechanisms and coping strategies that run your life on autopilot.

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. Which defense mechanism did I use today?
  2. What was I actually feeling underneath it?
  3. What would it look like to respond without the armor?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
