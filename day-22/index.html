Daily Read
The Reticular Activating System (RAS) 5 min

Your brain has a filter. It only shows you what you're focused on.

Buy a red car? Suddenly you see red cars everywhere. Focus on problems? You'll only see problems. Focus on opportunities? Your brain literally reorganizes to find them.

Vision boards and future-self journaling aren't magical thinking — they're programming your RAS.

"Whatever you hold in your mind on a consistent basis is exactly what you will experience in your life." — Tony Robbins

Daily Action
Create Your Vision 30 min

Your Assignment

Create a vision board (digital or physical). OR write a detailed description of your life 1 year from now. Where you live. What you do daily. Who's in your life. How you feel when you wake up. Be specific.

  • Create vision board OR write 1-year vision in detail
  • Include: where you live, what you do, who's around
  • Include: how you feel, what your morning looks like
  • Read it out loud or display it somewhere visible

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. What does my ideal life actually look like?
  2. What's the gap between where I am and where I want to be?
  3. What ONE thing could I start tomorrow that would close that gap fastest?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
