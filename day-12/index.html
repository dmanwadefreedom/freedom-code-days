Daily Read
Dopamine Addiction: Why You Can't Stop Scrolling 5 min

Social media, porn, sugar, gambling, shopping — all hijack dopamine.

Your baseline drops. You need MORE stimulation to feel NORMAL. This is why you're tired, unmotivated, and numbed out.

A dopamine detox isn't deprivation — it's recalibration. Boredom is the doorway to creativity. Sit with the discomfort.

"Almost everything will work again if you unplug it for a few minutes... including you." — Anne Lamott

Daily Action
4-Hour Dopamine Fast 4 hours

Your Assignment

4-hour dopamine fast. No phone, no social media, no TV, no sugar, no music. Journal what comes up. The discomfort IS the medicine.

  • Set a 4-hour window for your dopamine fast
  • No phone, social media, TV, sugar, or music
  • Journal every thought and feeling that comes up
  • Write about your primary love language

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

No screens after this. Grab your journal. Answer these 3 questions honestly.

  1. What was the hardest part of the dopamine fast?
  2. What thoughts/feelings surfaced when I removed distractions?
  3. What's my primary love language?

Write it down. Don't just think about it. Ink on paper changes the brain differently than thoughts in your head.
