Taking Inventory

Stop the bleeding. Build the morning anchor. Get honest.

Morning Protocol
Cold Exposure + Box Breathing 10 min

Before you check your phone. Before coffee. Before anything.

Cold water exposure (5 min): Cold shower if you have one. If not, fill a bowl with cold water and ice — submerge your face and hands for 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off. Repeat 5 times. This activates your vagus nerve and resets your nervous system.

Box breathing (3 rounds): Inhale 4 seconds. Hold 4 seconds. Exhale 4 seconds. Hold 4 seconds. That's 1 round. Do 3 rounds. Eyes closed. Spine straight.

Your body leads. Your mind follows.

Daily Read
Sugar: The Silent Hijacker 5 min

The average American eats 73 grams of sugar daily.

Sugar triggers the same dopamine pathways as cocaine. It causes brain fog, mood crashes, inflammation, gut damage, and hormonal chaos.

When you eat sugar, your blood glucose spikes, insulin floods, you crash, cravings repeat. This cycle keeps you in survival mode. Your brain literally cannot think clearly while riding the sugar rollercoaster.

Today: read every label. Count your grams.

Daily Action
Food Journal 15 min

Your Assignment

Write down everything you eat today. No judgment. Just awareness.

  • Track every meal and snack
  • Read food labels — count sugar grams
  • Write total sugar intake at end of day
  • Notice what emotion you felt before eating

Evening Reflection
Before You Sleep 5 min

You counted the grams. Now count the cost. Journal before bed.

  1. How many grams of sugar did I eat today?
  2. What emotion was I feeling before I reached for food?
  3. What would I eat differently tomorrow?

Write it. Your future self will thank you.
